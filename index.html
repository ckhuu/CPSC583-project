Side-by-side bars showing stats from the American Community Survey 2010-2012 Public Use Microdata Series, which contains data of recent college graduates.
For each major, each column displays proportional data of the proportion of female graduates, median income (in comparison to the maximum median of $110,000 in the dataset), proportion of graduates employed full-time, proportion of graduates with a job that requires a degree, and the unemployment rate.

How to use the visualization:

  • Select a major category from the drop-down below to filter by specific categories of study, or select "All major" to display the full data set.
  • To sort by ascending or descending order of your column of interest, click on a button below the column header.
  • Having trouble seeing the numbers on the bar? Hover over a bar to see this information.
